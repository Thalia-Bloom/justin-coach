What Is HYROX?

HYROX is a fitness race that combines 8km of running with 8 functional workout stations. It's challenging but accessible — and increasingly popular worldwide.

This program prepares you specifically for the demands of race day, so you can finish strong instead of just surviving.

Who It's For

This program is for first-time HYROX competitors or those who want to improve their finish time. You should have a baseline level of fitness — able to run 5K and do basic gym movements.
