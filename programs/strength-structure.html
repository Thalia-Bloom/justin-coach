What This Program Is

Strength & Structure is a 12-week progressive program focused on building functional strength through compound movements. It's designed for people who want to get stronger without spending hours in the gym.

Each week builds on the last. By the end, you'll have established a training foundation that you can build on for years.

Program Structure

Weeks 1–4 Foundation: Movement patterns and baseline strength
Weeks 5–8 Build: Progressive overload and volume
Weeks 9–12 Peak: Intensity and consolidation

What's Included

  • 12 weeks of structured programming (3–4 sessions/week)
  • Video demonstrations for every movement
  • Warm-up and mobility routines
  • Progress tracking spreadsheet
  • Nutrition guidelines (not a strict diet)
  • Email support throughout the program

Who It's For

This program is ideal for beginners to intermediate lifters who want structure without complexity. You'll need access to basic gym equipment (barbell, dumbbells, bench, rack).
